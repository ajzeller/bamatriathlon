Course Overview

Swim

The swim will be a 400m indoor pool swim in the UA Aquatic center pool. It will be a snake style swim with a rolling swim start where althetes begin according to their predicted swim time. After exiting the pool, athletes will exit the aquatic center and enter the transition area in the aquatic center parking lot to begin the bike portion of the race.

Bike

The bike course is a hybrid out-and-back and circuit-style course. After passing the UA Rec Center, athletes will turn left onto Campus Drive East and ride two loops as shown in the map. After the second loop, athletes will turn left onto 5th Ave E and return to the transition area. All corners will be monitored by police and race staff to ensure safety.

Run

The run course is a simple loop that provides a beautiful tour of the UA campus, beginning with a climb up towards the UA Rec Center and then descending back down through campus, passing by the UA quad and Bryant Denny Stadium before returning to the finish line at the transition area in the Aquatic center parking lot.

Race Day Information

Sunday, March 25th 2016 8am UA Aquatic Center

Athletes and spectators can park at UA Aquatic Center parking lot on Paul W Bryant Drive. Race day packet pickup will be available next to the transition area in the Aquatic center parking lot.
